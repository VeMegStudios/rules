VM Craft Network Rules

Rules

  • No Chat Abuse: Abusive language, inappropriate topics or jokes, misleading information, harassing or blackmailing others, sharing personal information, or sending malicious links, or any kind of useless chat spamming.
  • No Advertising: Advertising third-party websites, servers, discord names/tags, social media.
  • No Unfair Advantages: Any use of hacks, cheats, griefing, duping or unauthorized modifications is strictly prohibited.
  • No Glitches or Bugs Abuse: Report any bugs or glitches to the staff instead of exploiting them for personal gain.
  • No Inappropriate Cosmetics: Ensure that your character's skin, username, pet names, signs, item names are appropriate and do not violate server rules.
  • No AFK Farming: Automated systems that allow players to gain resources while AFK are not allowed.
  • No X-Ray Texture Packs: The use of texture packs or mods that allow x-ray vision or unfair advantages is prohibited.
  • No Client Modifications: Use only approved mods or client versions. Optifine and similar performance-enhancing mods are usually acceptable.
  • No Spawn Camping: Do not camp at spawn points to attack or kill new players.
  • Respect Building Guidelines: No offensive builds or big redstone builds.
  • Reporting Issues: Reporting players, bugs or glitches helps our team allows us to fix and improve the gameplay experience around and a better environment.
  • Respect Staff: Follow the instructions of server staff members and respect their decisions, and allow them to do their jobs.
